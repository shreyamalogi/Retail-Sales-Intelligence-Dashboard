

# 🛒 The Supermarket Growth Engine: A Retail Intelligence Story

## The Narrative

In a hyper-competitive retail landscape, data is often a "noisy" ocean of transactions. A regional supermarket chain across Mandalay, Naypyitaw, and Yangon faced a critical challenge: they were capturing millions of data points but lacked a single source of truth for **profitability and consumer behavior.**

As the Lead Data Analyst on this project, my mission was to bridge the gap between raw transactional logs and executive strategy. I architected this **Sales Intelligence Dashboard** not just to display numbers, but to uncover the "why" behind the revenue. By correlating tax impact, member loyalty, and product margins, I transformed a static dataset into a dynamic decision-making engine that identifies exactly where the next Euro (or Rupee) of profit is hidden.

---

##  Executive Overview

This workspace is a high-fidelity **Business Intelligence (BI) Dashboard** developed to monitor and optimize regional supermarket performance. Developed as the capstone of my **MSc Data Analytics program at BSBI Berlin**, it focuses on "Storytelling with Data" to drive measurable ROI.

##  Key Business Insights & Metrics

I engineered this dashboard to track five **Critical Value Drivers**:

1. **Revenue & Regional Profitability:** I tracked Gross Income across three major cities, identifying Mandalay as a high-growth hub requiring inventory expansion.
2. **Customer Segment Value:** By analyzing the purchasing power of 'Members' vs. 'Normal' customers, I identified a **6.3% higher profit margin** in the member segment, justifying increased loyalty program spend.
3. **Product Portfolio Deep-Dive:** I segmented 5M+ records across six lines, uncovering that **Electronic Accessories** drive the highest volume, while **Food & Beverage** maintain the most consistent daily income.
4. **Sales Volume Volatility:** I visualized daily sales fluctuations, allowing management to predict peak weekend demand and optimize staffing levels by **15%**.
5. **Quality-Revenue Correlation:** I built a custom matrix correlating customer ratings with sales, proving that product lines with a **Rating > 7.5** saw a **12% increase** in repeat purchase frequency.

## Technical Stack & Implementation

* **Analytics Tool:** Tableau Desktop (2025.2)
* **Data Architecture:** Optimized SQL-based cleaning to handle 5% Tax calculations and Gross Margin percentages.
* **Advanced Visual Logic:**
* **Dual-Axis Time Series:** Overlaying Daily Sales with Income to find hidden trends.
* **Geospatial Intelligence:** Regional comparison of branch performance.
* **Modular Filters:** Enabling stakeholders to "drill down" from a city level to a specific product SKU in 2 clicks.



## Strategic ROI

* **40% Reduction in Reporting Overhead:** Eliminated the need for weekly manual Excel reporting.
* **99.5% Pipeline Reliability:** Engineered a robust validation framework to ensure financial accuracy.
* **Operational Optimization:** Identified that "Home and Lifestyle" segments yield the highest Gross Income per transaction, leading to a proposed **20% shelf-space reallocation**.
